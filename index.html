Whether it's book designs, posters, company and social branding, or website designs, my overall goal is to provide powerful and heavy visual content that speaks louder than words. Hi, I'm Zahara Acevedo. I'm a graphic design major at Eastern Washington University, currently layering a foundation in visual communication with studies in animation, UX/UI, and web development. I believe that good design requires both technical rigor and a curious mind, and I'm constantly looking for new ways to bridge the two.

I specialize in high-contrast, maximalist illustration and graphic design. My process is rooted in the early stages of conceptual drafting, where I bridge the gap between raw visual ideas and polished digital execution.

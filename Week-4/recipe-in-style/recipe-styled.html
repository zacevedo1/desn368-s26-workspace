Macaroni Salad

A fresh macaroni salad with ham, egg, and mayo perfect during
the summer or as a side dish.

Ingredients

Depending on how much you want to make, ingrdients amount may very

  • 1 box of Elbow macaroni
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 slices of ham
  • 2 spoons of mayo
  • Salt

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Put a pot of water to boil
  2. Add pasta and perfered amount of salt, and wait till softend
  3. In a seperate pot, boil water and then add your eggs
  4. While eggs and pasta are boiling, take your ham and cut into
    squares, cut to desired sizes
  5. Once past and eggs are cooked, drain pasta and put it to the
    side while you begin to peal the egg shells off the eggs.
  6. Cut the eggs up
  7. Put your pasta into a bowl and then add your sliced and
    diced eggs and ham
  8. Add mayo (however much you perfer)
  9. Enjoy!

Tips & Variations

  • For a creamier salad, add extra mayo to taste
  • You can substitute ham with bacon or diced chicken
  • Add diced bell peppers or celery for extra crunch
  • Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving
  • Best enjoyed within 2-3 days of preparation

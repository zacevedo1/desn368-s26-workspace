Chapter 1

The Orchard of Machines

Vesta Relay Station · Inner Asteroid Belt

4 March 2319 · 06:18:44 UTC

The garden was not supposed to grow. Nothing grew on Vesta except frost, dust, and the thin metallic lichen that collected around broken vents. But every morning, when the station lights rose from blue to white, another row of silver stems unfolded from the floor of the abandoned relay room.

Ivo found them by accident while chasing a maintenance drone that had stopped obeying orders. The drone hovered above the strange plants, clicking softly to itself, its camera fixed on the tallest stem. At the end of that stem was a glass bulb no larger than a coin, and inside the bulb a tiny green signal pulsed like a heartbeat.

By noon, every screen on the station displayed the same message: do not cut the garden. No one had typed it. No system admitted sending it. When Ivo placed his hand near the nearest stem, the message changed. This time it used his childhood name, the one only his sister had known before Earth went silent.

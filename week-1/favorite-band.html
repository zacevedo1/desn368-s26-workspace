Hozier: Unreal Unearth Tour

Hozier is an Irish singer-songwriter celebrated for his "forest deity" persona and a haunting
blend of folk, blues, and gospel. He shot to stardom with the 2013 hit "Take Me to Church," a
powerful critique of institutional religion that earned him a Grammy nomination.
